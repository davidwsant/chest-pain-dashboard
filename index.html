Background

The introduction of EHRs has helped physicians greatly and allowed for patients to have their records transfered from one clinic to another, but they have also led to higher rates of job dissatisfaction and physician burnout. One possible solution to fixing alleviating this burden in the Emergency Department would be to create chief-complaint dashboards that allow the provider to click a single button and have all of the information provided on a single screen. This website will show you an example of how this would look in an EHR designed after the layout of EPIC. Four patients have been designed for testing purposes. All patients are entirely fictitous and any resemblence of real people, alive or dead, is entirely coincidental.
